Beefalo Taming Guide

Why Tame a Beefalo?

Taming a Beefalo makes *Don't Starve Together* easier with these perks:

  • Faster Travel: Move quicker, even with heavy items.
  • Strong Attacks: Deal up to 68 damage, like a dark sword.
  • Tough Shield: 1000 health, takes hits for you, heals 6.9 health every 10 seconds.
  • Free Fuel: Manure for fire pits all year.

It takes 15–20 days, but you can tame while playing normally.

How to Tame

Simple steps to tame your Beefalo:

  1. Find One: Check Savanna or Mandrake Forest. Use a Beefalo Bell (crafted at a Science Machine) to bond.
  2. Feed Often: Feed to keep obedience above 50% for riding. Use petals or light bulbs (cheap) or steamed twigs (1 twig + 3 fillers in a crockpot).
  3. Ride or Feed: Ride or keep hunger above 0 to gain 5% domestication daily (20 days total). Brushing speeds it up to 15 days.
  4. Use a Salt Lick: Place a Salt Lick (2 boards + 4 nitre, Alchemy Engine) within 4m to pause domestication loss. At full durability, a Salt Lick can keep a Beefalo from losing domestication for approximately 15 days, at 16 uses per day with 240 durability.
  5. Pick a Type:
    • Rider: Fastest, great for travel.
    • Ornery: High damage, good for fights.
    • Pudgy: Boosts sanity, but slow.
    • Default: Balanced, no special perks.

Tip: Don’t attack your Beefalo (loses 30% domestication) or overfeed (loses 1% if hunger exceeds 375).

How to Force a Beefalo Type

During taming, you can influence your Beefalo’s tendency to become Rider, Ornery, Pudgy, or Default by your actions:

  • Rider (Speed): Ride the Beefalo as much as possible without fighting. Avoid combat to prioritize speed tendency. Use a Glossamer Saddle to reinforce this.
  • Ornery (Combat): Engage in combat while riding. Attack enemies to increase the combat tendency. Equip a War Saddle or Nightmare Saddle to boost this.
  • Pudgy (Sanity): Overfeed the Beefalo by keeping its hunger above 375 as often as possible. Use high-hunger foods like steamed twigs or crops. This increases the Pudgy tendency.
  • Default (Balanced): Avoid extreme actions. Feed and ride moderately without focusing on combat or overfeeding to maintain a balanced tendency.

Tip: Check the Beefalo’s face periodically (e.g., smiley for Rider, angry for Ornery) to confirm the tendency. Adjust your actions if needed before full domestication.

Gear to Use

Boost your Beefalo with these:

  • Default Saddle: 40% speed boost.
  • Glossamer Saddle: 55% speed boost.
  • War Saddle: +16 damage, mount in combat.
  • Battle Saddle (Wigfrid): +5 damage, 40% less damage taken.
  • Nightmare Saddle: +18 planar damage, 60% less damage taken.
  • Gloom Bell: Revives dead Beefalo (2-hour cooldown).
