Beefalo Taming Guide

Why Tame a Beefalo?

Taming a Beefalo in Don't Starve Together provides three key benefits:

  • Speed: Move faster than a player, even with heavy objects.
  • Infinite Weapon: Deal up to 68 damage per attack when fully tamed, matching a dark sword.
  • Meat Shield: 1000 health, absorbs melee damage, regenerates 6.9 health every 10 seconds.

Plus, their manure fuels fire pits year-round! Taming takes 15–20 days but can be done alongside regular gameplay.

How to Tame a Beefalo

Follow these steps to tame your Beefalo efficiently:

  1. Find and Bond: Locate Beefalo in Savanna or Mandrake Forest biomes. Craft a Beefalo Bell at a Science Machine to bond, making the Beefalo follow you.
  2. Manage Stats:
    • Obedience: Keep ≥50% to ride (feed to increase by 10%, decays over time or by 1% per hit).
    • Domestication: Gain 5% daily by riding or keeping hunger >0 (20 days to 100%). Brushing speeds to 15 days.
    • Tendency: Determines final stats (Rider: speed, Ornery: damage, Pudgy: sanity, Default: balanced).
  3. Feed Smart: Use steamed twigs (1 twig + 3 fillers in a crockpot) for 100 hunger. Avoid overfeeding (hunger >375 causes 1% domestication loss).

Gear and Equipment

Enhance your Beefalo with these items:

  • Default Saddle: 40% speed boost.
  • Glossamer Saddle: 55% speed boost (68 butterfly wings).
  • War Saddle: 25% speed, +16 damage, combat saddle.
  • Battle Saddle (Wigfrid): 30% speed, +5 damage, 40% damage reduction.
  • Nightmare Saddle: 45% speed, +18 planar damage, 60% physical + 15 planar damage reduction.
  • Beefalo Gloom Bell: Revives dead Beefalo (2-hour cooldown).
  • Grooming Station: Keeps Beefalo stationary at base.

Practical Tips

  • Feed light bulbs/petals to boost obedience cheaply before riding.
  • Reset the buck timer by feeding while riding to avoid dismounts in danger.
  • In spring, trap Beefalo on a boat with steamed twigs or salt licks to avoid heat hostility.
  • Monitor tendency via the Beefalo’s face; use a punching bag for safe Ornery points.
  • Drop the Beefalo Bell far from bosses to prevent aggro.
  • Pair with characters like Wendy (damage boost) or Wormwood (poop for health).
  • Use a salt lick bug: Lick once, ride away, and domestication loss pauses until cave switch.
